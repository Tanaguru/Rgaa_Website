Website audience analytics

We use the Matomo service to measure the audience of this website.

Statistics are collected in accordance with the rules set out by the CNIL:

  • anonymization of IP addresses (only the first two bytes of IP addresses are kept);
  • implementation of the unsubscription solution (below);
  • application of a maximum lifetime of 13 months for tracking cookies;
  • support for the "Do Not Track" preference. You will not be followed if your browser transmits that you do not want to be followed. This is a setting in your browser and you will not be able to participate until you disable the "do not follow" feature.

This information will never be transmitted to a third party.

You can unsubscribe using the box below (uncheck the box to stop being tracked if it is available).
